# Company Onboarding Questionnaire

Welcome, and thank you for choosing Mind Measure to support your people.

This questionnaire helps us set up your organisation on the Mind Measure
platform so that we can have everything ready for the day your pilot
launches. It should take roughly **30 minutes** to complete with the right
people in the room — typically HR, with an occasional dip into IT for
section 5.

Please return your answers to your Mind Measure account manager once
complete. If you're unsure of an answer, leave it blank and we'll discuss it
during your kick-off call.

## 8. If Mind Measure detects a high-risk score, who should we route the employee to first?

When a check-in result suggests an employee may be struggling, the app
gently offers them a list of help options. By default we suggest:

1. Your line manager / HR business partner
2. Your EAP (24/7)
3. A Mental Health First Aider
4. A national crisis line
5. Emergency services (999 / 112 / local equivalent)

Would you like to keep this order, change it, or insert a company-specific
