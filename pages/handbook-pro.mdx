## 1. About Mind Measure Pro

Mind Measure Pro is a multimodal conversational analytics platform designed to support employee wellbeing in the workplace. Through a brief daily conversation with an AI companion called Jodie, employees receive a personalised wellbeing score that tracks their mental health over time — giving both the individual and their organisation a clearer picture of how they are feeling.

The platform is **not** a diagnostic tool, a replacement for counselling, or a clinical intervention. It is a structured, evidence-informed monitoring system that helps organisations identify emerging patterns, allocate resources more effectively, and support employees before small concerns become serious problems.

### What Mind Measure Pro Is

- A daily wellness check-in that takes 1–2 minutes
- A personal baseline assessment that establishes an individual's starting point
- A dashboard for employees to track their own wellbeing
- An organisational analytics platform for aggregated, anonymised insights
- A content hub for companies to share wellbeing resources and nudges

### What Mind Measure Pro Is Not

- A medical device or clinical diagnostic tool
- A replacement for professional mental health support
- A surveillance tool — organisations never see individual conversations
- An app that collects location data, contact lists, photos, or browsing history
- A performance management or HR monitoring system

## 2. Our Philosophy

Mind Measure Pro operates on a simple principle: **early awareness leads to better outcomes**. Most employees who struggle with their mental health do not seek help until they are in crisis. By providing a low-friction, non-judgemental way to reflect on how they are feeling each day, we aim to normalise the conversation around mental health and create a continuous feedback loop between employees and the support systems around them.

Our approach is grounded in three commitments:

**Privacy First** — Employees own their data. Individual conversations and scores are never shared with employers. Organisations receive only aggregated, anonymised analytics at team or company level.

**Evidence-Informed** — Our methodology draws on validated clinical screening instruments (PHQ-2, GAD-2) and peer-reviewed research into vocal biomarkers, facial expression analysis, and linguistic markers of wellbeing.

**Human-Centred Design** — Technology serves the conversation, not the other way around. Jodie's conversational style is warm, non-clinical, and adaptive. The experience is designed to feel like a brief check-in with a trusted companion, not a form to fill in.

## 3. How It Works

### The Employee Experience

#### Baseline Assessment
When an employee first uses Mind Measure Pro, they complete a one-off baseline assessment lasting 5–10 minutes. During this conversation, Jodie asks a series of validated clinical screening questions alongside more open-ended prompts. This establishes the employee's personal wellness baseline — a reference point against which all future check-ins are measured.

The clinical screening includes:
- **PHQ-2** — A two-question depression screening tool widely used in primary care (scored 0–6)
- **GAD-2** — A two-question generalised anxiety screening tool (scored 0–6)
- **Self-reported mood** — A single 1–10 scale question

These instruments are internationally validated and recommended by NICE and the American Psychiatric Association for initial screening.

#### Daily Check-ins
After the baseline, employees are encouraged to check in daily. Each check-in is a brief (1–2 minute) conversation with Jodie, who asks how they are feeling, follows up on themes from previous conversations, and gently explores what is going well and what might be troubling them.

The conversation is natural and adaptive — Jodie remembers context from previous check-ins and adjusts her questions accordingly. Employees speak naturally; there are no forms, scales, or multiple-choice questions in the daily check-in.

#### The Mind Measure Score
After each check-in, the platform computes a Mind Measure Score on a 0–100 scale. This score reflects the employee's overall wellbeing based on a proprietary multimodal analysis of their conversation. The score is displayed on their personal dashboard alongside trends, themes, and positive drivers.

**Score ranges:**
| Range | Label | Indicator |
|-------|-------|-----------|
| 80–100 | Thriving | Green |
| 60–79 | Steady | Blue |
| 40–59 | Building momentum | Amber |
| 0–39 | Support available | Red |

Employees can also see:
- **Mood trend** — A 7-day bar chart of self-reported mood
- **Check-in streak** — How many consecutive days they have checked in
- **Insight, enjoying, and worry summaries** — What they talked about in recent check-ins
- **Previous check-in detail** — A comparison with their last conversation

#### Nudges
Companies can send short, text-based nudges to employees through the app. These are brief notifications directing employees to available support — an EAP workshop on managing workload, a wellbeing drop-in session, a financial advice clinic, or a resilience seminar. Nudges are prioritised by urgency and displayed as a swipeable carousel on the employee dashboard.

#### Cohort-Targeted Content
Both nudges and articles can be targeted to specific employee cohorts. When creating content, administrators choose between "Whole Company" and "Specific Cohort". In cohort mode, cascading dropdowns allow selection by department, role/team, seniority, office location, and work mode. The system displays a live count of matching employees, so the administrator can see exactly how many people will receive the content.

This feature connects directly to the cohort analytics dashboards. If an administrator identifies a wellbeing trend in a particular group — for example, elevated stress in the Sales team during quarter-end — they can immediately create targeted support content that only reaches that specific group, without generating noise for the rest of the organisation.

Content without targeting continues to be visible to all employees.

#### Pinning Content
Any nudge or article can be pinned to the top of the employee feed until a specified date. This is useful for time-sensitive content such as benefits enrollment deadlines, wellbeing week events, or urgent policy communications. When the pin date passes, the content automatically returns to its normal position in the feed — no manual intervention required.

### The Organisational Experience

#### Company Dashboard
Company administrators access the organisational dashboard at a secure URL. This provides aggregated, anonymised analytics across the employee population, including:

- **Team wellbeing scores** — Average scores by department, team, location, or custom cohort
- **Trend analysis** — How scores are changing over time
- **Engagement metrics** — Check-in frequency, active user counts
- **Top themes** — What employees are talking about (aggregated and anonymised)
- **Risk indicators** — Proportion of employees in each score zone

No individual employee data is visible to organisational users. All analytics are based on aggregated data with a minimum cohort size to prevent identification.

#### Content Hub
The Company Content Hub is a companion platform where administrators can:

- **Create and publish wellbeing articles** — Using a rich text editor to produce blog-style content
- **Manage nudges** — Create, schedule, and prioritise short notifications for employees
- **Target content to specific cohorts** — Direct articles and nudges to particular employee groups by department, role, seniority, office, or work mode
- **Pin content to the top of the feed** — Ensure time-sensitive material is seen first, with automatic expiry
- **Browse the content pool** — Access articles shared by other organisations and by Mind Measure
- **View employee insights** — See aggregated "Top 10" employee concerns and interests to inform content strategy

The Content Hub is accessed directly from the organisational dashboard with seamless authentication — no separate login required.

## 4. The Science Behind Mind Measure

### Multimodal Analysis

Mind Measure uses a multimodal approach to wellbeing assessment, combining three complementary data streams:

**Linguistic Analysis** — What employees say, and how they say it. The platform analyses sentiment, word choice, engagement, coherence, and the balance of positive and negative language. Specific patterns associated with depression, anxiety, and stress are identified using natural language processing.

**Vocal Analysis** — How employees sound. Research has established that vocal characteristics including pitch variability, speaking rate, pause patterns, voice energy, and vocal quality are associated with emotional state and wellbeing. These features are extracted and analysed in real time.

**Visual Analysis** — How employees present. Facial expression analysis examines indicators such as smile frequency, eye contact, emotional valence, and overall engagement. These signals complement the linguistic and vocal data.

Each modality contributes to the final Mind Measure Score through a proprietary fusion algorithm. The weighting is designed to prioritise the most reliable and clinically relevant signals while gracefully handling situations where one or more modalities may be unavailable (for example, if an employee's camera is off).

### Research Foundation

The assessment methodology draws on established research in:

- **Clinical screening** — PHQ-2 and GAD-2 are validated screening instruments with extensive evidence bases
- **Vocal biomarkers** — Peer-reviewed studies linking vocal characteristics to depression, anxiety, and stress
- **Facial expression analysis** — Research on facial action coding systems and their relationship to emotional state
- **Linguistic markers** — Studies on language patterns associated with psychological distress

### Important Limitations

- The Mind Measure Score is **not a clinical diagnosis**. It is a wellness indicator that should be interpreted alongside professional judgement
- Audio and visual features are research-informed but have not yet undergone independent clinical validation as standalone diagnostic instruments
- Technical factors (device quality, internet connection, ambient noise, lighting) can affect the quality of multimodal analysis
- The platform requires regular use to build a meaningful picture — isolated check-ins should be interpreted with caution
- Fusion algorithm weights represent initial calibration and will be refined through ongoing longitudinal validation

### Ongoing Research

Mind Measure is committed to continuous validation and improvement. Research priorities include:
- Longitudinal validation against established clinical measures (PHQ-9, GAD-7)
- Population norms across different demographics and organisations
- Algorithmic fairness and bias auditing across diverse populations
- Refinement of scoring weights based on outcome data

## 5. Security and Privacy

### Our Security Philosophy

Mind Measure handles sensitive wellbeing data. We treat this responsibility with the same rigour as clinical systems, implementing medical-grade security controls throughout the platform.

**Defence in Depth** — Multiple overlapping security layers ensure that no single failure compromises employee data. Encryption, access controls, monitoring, and audit logging all work together.

**Least Privilege** — Every user, system, and service has access only to the minimum data required for their function. Company administrators cannot access individual employee conversations. Aggregated data requires minimum cohort sizes.

**Privacy by Design** — Data minimisation is built into every feature. We collect only what is necessary, retain it only as long as required, and delete it when it is no longer needed.

**Employer Separation** — Mind Measure Pro is explicitly designed so that employers cannot identify, monitor, or target individual employees based on their wellbeing data. The platform is a benefit, not a surveillance tool.

### Technical Security Measures

| Layer | Implementation |
|-------|---------------|
| **Encryption in transit** | TLS 1.3 for all communications |
| **Encryption at rest** | AES-256 for all stored data |
| **Authentication** | Multi-factor authentication required for all administrative access |
| **Access control** | Role-based access control with granular permissions |
| **Audit logging** | Comprehensive, tamper-proof audit trails for all data access |
| **Monitoring** | Real-time security monitoring with automated alerting |
| **Backup** | Daily automated backups with 35-day retention and point-in-time recovery |
| **Disaster recovery** | Recovery time objective under 6 hours, recovery point objective under 15 minutes |

### Compliance

Mind Measure is designed to meet the requirements of:

- **UK GDPR** — Full compliance with data subject rights including access, rectification, erasure, and portability
- **HIPAA** — Administrative, physical, and technical safeguards for protected health information
- **SOC 2 Type II** — Controls for security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy

### What Data Is Collected

| Data Type | Purpose | Retention |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| Account information (email, name) | Authentication and personalisation | Duration of account |
| Company affiliation | Routing to correct organisational dashboard | Duration of account |
| Assessment responses and scores | Wellbeing tracking and trend analysis | As required by data retention policy |
| Audio data | Real-time vocal analysis only | Processed and deleted within 24 hours |
| Aggregated analytics | Organisational reporting | As required |

### What Is Never Collected

- Precise location data
- Contact lists or address books
- Photos or videos from the device (camera is used only during live conversation, no recordings stored)
- Other app usage or browsing history
- Any data used for advertising
- Job title, performance reviews, or HR data
- Any data shared with line managers, HR, or other employees

### Employee Privacy Rights

Under UK GDPR, employees have the right to:
- **Access** their personal data
- **Rectify** inaccurate data
- **Erase** their data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Port** their data to another service
- **Object** to specific processing

## 6. Platform Architecture

### Overview

Mind Measure Pro is built on a cloud-native architecture designed for security, scalability, and reliability:

- **Mobile Application** — A progressive web application wrapped in a native iOS container, providing a native app experience with web deployment flexibility
- **Organisational Dashboard** — A secure web application for company administrators
- **Content Hub** — A standalone web application for content and nudge management
- **Backend Services** — Hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS), providing enterprise-grade infrastructure
- **Database** — Amazon Aurora Serverless, providing automatic scaling and high availability

### Infrastructure

The platform runs entirely on AWS and Vercel infrastructure:

- **Compute** — Serverless functions that scale automatically with demand
- **Database** — Aurora Serverless v2 PostgreSQL with automatic scaling, encryption, and multi-availability-zone deployment
- **Storage** — Amazon S3 with AES-256 encryption for all stored objects
- **AI Services** — Amazon Bedrock for text analysis, Amazon Rekognition for visual analysis
- **Conversational AI** — ElevenLabs multimodal engine for natural conversation
- **Content Delivery** — Amazon CloudFront CDN for fast, secure content delivery
- **Authentication** — Amazon Cognito with multi-factor authentication

### Scalability

The architecture is designed to scale from a single company pilot to enterprise-wide deployment without architectural changes. Key scalability features include:
- Automatic database scaling from 0.5 to 16 capacity units based on demand
- Serverless compute that handles up to 1,000 concurrent assessment sessions
- CDN caching for fast content delivery globally
- Cost allocation per company for transparent billing

## 8. Company Onboarding

### Getting Started

Onboarding a new company involves:

1. **Account Setup** — Creation of organisational account with appropriate access levels
2. **Configuration** — Branding, team structure, and resource setup
3. **Staff Training** — Walkthrough of the organisational dashboard and content hub
4. **Employee Launch** — Communication materials and app distribution
5. **Ongoing Support** — Regular check-ins and analytics review

### Staff Roles

| Role | Access Level |
|------|-------------|
| **Company Admin** | Full dashboard, content hub, user management |
| **Wellbeing Lead** | Dashboard analytics, content creation, nudge management |
| **Team Lead** | Team-level analytics (filtered view) |

### Integration

Mind Measure Pro operates as a standalone platform and does not require integration with existing company systems. Employees register directly through the app using their work email address. The platform automatically associates employees with their organisation.

For enterprises requiring deeper integration, Mind Measure Pro supports:
- **SSO** — Single sign-on via SAML 2.0 or OpenID Connect (available on request)
- **Anonymised Data API** — Aggregated analytics export for integration with existing HR wellbeing platforms (available on request)
