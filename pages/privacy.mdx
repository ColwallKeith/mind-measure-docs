# Privacy & Legal

## Overview

Mind Measure maintains strict privacy and legal compliance standards across all platforms and jurisdictions.

## Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: January 2025  
**Version**: 2.0

Mind Measure is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of your personal and health information. This app processes sensitive health data related to mental wellness following HIPAA, UK GDPR, and other applicable privacy regulations.

### Information We Collect

**Personal Information**
- Account Information: Name, email address, password
- Profile Data: Display name, avatar, wellness goals, preferences
- University Affiliation: Institution code (for cohort assignment only)

**Health and Wellness Data**
- Assessment Responses: Wellness questionnaires and voice assessments
- Mood Tracking: Daily mood check-ins and self-reported wellness indicators
- Audio Data: Voice recordings (processed and deleted within 24 hours)
- Usage Patterns: App interaction patterns, session duration, feature usage

**What We DON'T Collect**
- Precise Location tracking
- Contact list access
- Photos/Videos from your device
- Other app usage monitoring

### How We Use Your Information

1. **Personal Wellness Tracking**: Provide personalized mental health insights
2. **Baseline Assessment**: Establish your personal wellness baseline
3. **Progress Monitoring**: Track your wellness journey over time
4. **Crisis Prevention**: Identify concerning patterns and provide resources
5. **App Improvement**: Enhance features based on usage patterns

### Data Sharing and Security

**We Share Information With:**
- **Service Providers**: Supabase (EU-hosted), OpenAI (transcription), AWS (processing)
- **University Partners**: Aggregated, anonymized data only - never individual data
- **Emergency Services**: Only when legally required for immediate safety

**Security Measures:**
- All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- Multi-factor authentication and role-based access
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- 24-hour breach notification procedures

### Your Privacy Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your data
- **Portability**: Receive your data in machine-readable format
- **Objection**: Object to processing based on legitimate interest

Contact: privacy@mindmeasure.app
